Top 5 Highest/Farthest Jumping Animals

(compared to their size)

1. Flea

Fleas can jump 250 meters times their own body length and 150 times their own body height. That would be like us jumping 400 meters in length while jumping over a 250 meter high building!

2. Tree Frog

Tree Frogs can jump 150 times their own body length.

3. Jumping Spider

Jumping Spiders can jump 100 times their own body length.

4. Froghopper

Froghoppers can jump 70 times their own body height.

5. Kangaroo Rat

Kangaroo Rats can jump 45 times their own body length
